Founded in 1996, the Tritones is UC San Diego’s premiere vocal ensemble. The Tritones sing “a cappella,” that is: without any instrumental help save for our own voices. We are a diverse group of individuals with academic interests scattered throughout the disciplines & extracurricular involvements just as varied. Although our members are very different, we are all united in a passion for singing. In recent years, we’ve hosted annual Fall and Spring Ballroom shows, won the Los Angeles A Cappella Festival, and advanced as far as the semi-finals in the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella. Our members have shared the stage with many renowned performers including Taylor Swift, Far East Movement, and Imogen Heap.
